2.3. pctheory.group

This module contains a class representing a group of operators.

class OperatorGroup

Represents a group of TnMm operators, as explained in chapter 4 of Robert Morris's Composition with Pitch Classes: A Theory of Compositional Design. Operator groups are mathematical groups; that is, they have an operation (operator concatenation), they have an identity element (T0M1), they are associative, and for every TnMm in the group, there is an inverse (TnMm)‐1 in the group. The TTOs (twelve-tone operators) are a mathematical group, as well as smaller subgroups of the TTOs.

OperatorGroup constructor

OperatorGroup.__init__(utos: list = None)

Constructs an OperatorGroup. You can initialize the group with a list of UTOs that form a group.

OperatorGroup read-only properties

OperatorGroup.namestr

Gets the name of the operator group. For example, the group of all TTOs is G*/*/*/*.

OperatorGroup methods

OperatorGroup overloaded special functions

Overloaded special functions include __repr__() and __str__(). The str() implementation allows RotationalArray objects to be printed.
